Who We Are

A Professional Consulting Practice for International Business

Nordic Riser AB is an international consulting firm specialising in strategic business establishment and structured professional relocation in Europe. We work with entrepreneurs, independent professionals, and business owners who are building a credible, legally compliant presence in European markets.

Our work is advisory in nature. We do not provide legal representation or immigration services. Instead, we offer structured strategic guidance — combined with coordination of qualified professionals across legal, accounting, and regulatory disciplines — to ensure that every process our clients undertake is built on solid, defensible foundations.

"To provide strategic consulting services that help international entrepreneurs establish legally structured and sustainable business operations in Europe."

Why We Were Created

Built to Address a Genuine Gap in International Advisory

We recognised that too many internationally minded professionals were navigating complex European business and relocation processes without access to structured, professional-grade strategic support.

The Problem We Solve

International entrepreneurs and professionals often encounter fragmented advice — generic information that fails to account for their specific business model, professional background, or long-term objectives. The result is poor planning, avoidable delays, and structures that do not withstand institutional scrutiny.

Nordic Riser AB was established to change this. We bring a consulting firm's rigour to what is often an unstructured process — delivering clarity, documentation, and professional coordination at every stage.

Our focus is always on long-term strategy and sustainable outcomes, not short-term convenience.

Our Approach

Strategy First. Structure Always. Compliance Throughout.

Our methodology is consistent across every engagement. We begin with strategy, build the appropriate legal and operational structure, and coordinate execution with licensed professionals.

01

Strategy Before Action

We assess your specific situation thoroughly before making any recommendations. Strategic clarity is the foundation of every successful engagement.

02

Legal Structure by Design

We identify the appropriate legal and corporate framework for your circumstances — entity type, jurisdiction, and compliance obligations are determined systematically.

03

Professional Guidance Only

We do not offer generic information. Every recommendation is specific, documented, and grounded in professional consulting practice.

04

Execution with Licensed Partners

Implementation is coordinated with qualified legal, accounting, and specialist professionals — ensuring technical accuracy and regulatory compliance at every step.

05

Long-Term Business Focus

We measure success by the sustainability of the structures we help create — not by the volume of engagements we process.

06

Transparent Communication

We provide honest, realistic assessments. We do not overstate what is achievable, and we communicate requirements clearly at every stage.
